Favorite Places on the Ave

Widely known amongst UW students as the "Ave", this street is actually University Way NE in West Campus.


Pho Shizzle

Pho Shizzle is a staple pho shop for everyone. Grab a comforting bowl of soup and eat enough bean sprouts until you explode!

4235 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105

Oasis Tea Zone

If you're at Oasis, you're at home. Another staple for the UW student when visiting the Ave. Check out their yummy popcorn chicken!

Oasis Tea Zone (U-District), 4524 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
